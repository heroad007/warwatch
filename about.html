Project Mission

Why WarWatch?

WarWatch was developed to address the difficulty of verifying real-time global conflict data. By utilizing advanced Web Engineering techniques, our platform provides:

  • Live Intelligence Feeds: Aggregating breaking news from verified operational theaters.
  • Interactive Geo-Mapping: Visualizing impact zones using dynamic GIS layers.
  • Data Integrity: Providing a structured, academic approach to conflict analysis.

This project serves as a comprehensive dataset and analytical tool for researchers, analysts, and policymakers interested in the dynamics of modern warfare.
