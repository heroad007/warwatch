About WarWatch Institute

WarWatch is an academic engineering initiative designed to provide verifiable, unbiased intelligence regarding ongoing global conflicts. By aggregating data on infrastructure loss, military expenditures, and geopolitical news, this platform serves as a central hub for conflict impact analysis. Developed as a B.Tech Web Engineering project, our methodology relies on combining real-time APIs with structured data archiving to visualize the true cost of war.
